## Design Context

### Users
Professional filmmakers, photographers, and production agencies looking for high-quality rental equipment and services.

### Brand Personality
Cinematográfica, limpia, minimalista.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark, high-end, visual-centric. Follows the official Maya Studios style (https://www.mayastudios.it/).

### Design Principles
- Visual Immersive: Large, high-quality product imagery.
- Minimalist Hierarchy: Clean typography, plenty of negative space.
- Fluid Motion: High-end transitions (cross-fades, smooth eases).
- Utilitarian Precision: Easy access to technical categories.
